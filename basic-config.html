Basic Configuration

For basic usage, add the plugin dependency to your POM:

<plugin>
    <groupId>com.zenjava</groupId>
    <artifactId>javafx-maven-plugin</artifactId>
    <version>8.1.2</version>
    <configuration>
        <mainClass>[put your application main class here]</mainClass>
        <vendor>[Vendor Name]</vendor>
    </configuration>
</plugin>

Note, some of the goals require a 'vendor' and in general you are generally just better off setting your organisation's name in your project's POM from the get-go and never having to worry about it:

<organization>
  <name>Your Organisation</name>
</organization>
